About Me


Who Am I?

My name is Kyle Ward and I'm a Computer Science major at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. My concentration is in AI, Robotics, and Gaming. I am also pursuing a minor in Computer Engineering. My true passion is Artificial Intelligence and I plan on one day, helping humanity achieve the technological singularity by creating the world's first superhuman AI.



How Did I Get Here?

It all began in my sophmore year of high school. That Christmas, I got my own gaming pc which sparked my interest in computers and how they work. This lead me to take a programming class the following semester. I instantly fell in love with everything computer science related and found that I had knack for it.

I found my passion for AI in my senior year of high school. One day, I was in class, bored, so I decided that I was going to try and make a chatbot using AI. Fast forward 8 months, and I had a fully-function chatbot that was perfectly fluent in gibberish and a newfound obsession with the wonderfully incomprehensible world of Artificial Intelligence!



What's Next?

At the time I'm writing this (March 2020), my goals for the future are relatively simple. I plan on finishing my B.S. degree at UNCC, and hopefully finding a job on the forefront of AI research and development. At the same time, I would also like to pursue a Master's degree. Then, eventually, I'm going to start my own tech company.
